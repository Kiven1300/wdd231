Membership Levels

NP Membership

Free

Available for non-profit organizations. Enjoy basic directory listing and access to chamber newsletters and community events.

  • Directory listing
  • Monthly newsletter
  • Community event access

Bronze Membership

$500 MXN / year

Entry-level membership for small businesses. Get listed in the directory and receive promotional support.

  • Directory listing
  • Monthly newsletter
  • 1 social media mention per year
  • Networking events access

Silver Membership

$1,500 MXN / year

Ideal for established businesses looking for more visibility and engagement with the Xochimilco business community.

  • All Bronze benefits
  • Spotlight on Home page
  • 4 social media mentions per year
  • Priority event seating

Gold Membership

$3,000 MXN / year

Maximum visibility and engagement for businesses that want to lead the local commerce community.

  • All Silver benefits
  • Featured spotlight (priority)
  • Monthly social media mentions
  • Logo on chamber homepage
  • Speaking opportunities at events
